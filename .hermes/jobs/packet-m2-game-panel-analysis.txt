ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK:

### 1. UI (界面)
- Is information hierarchy clear? Does the player know what to look at first?
- Are the panels appropriately sized? Is there too much scrolling?
- Is the current phase clearly indicated? Does the player know WHAT to do right now?
- Is feedback clear? (submitting a decision → visual confirmation)
- Color coding: are numbers that change (costs, gains) visually distinct?
- Are error messages clear when the player does something wrong?

### 2. 数值设计 (Number Design)
- Are resource numbers (govFiscal, militaryPoints, rawMaterials) clearly displayed?
- Are costs visible before the player commits to an action?
- Is the budget pool clear? Does the player understand what they're spending from?
- Are there misleading numbers? (e.g., showing a number that doesn't update until next turn)
- Turn/Round indicator: clear?

### 3. 元素设计 (Element Design)
- Are all sub-panels necessary? Any that could be merged?
- Is the player overwhelmed with choices? Does the UI guide them to what's important?
- Are there dead/never-used controls?
- Does the game theme (19th century geopolitics) come through in the UI?
- Is the transition between phases smooth?
